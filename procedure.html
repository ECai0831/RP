Step-by-Step Procedure


Step 1

Plant 5 Opuntia humifusa pads; maintain at 27°C daytime / 22°C nightime with a 16-hour daytime & generous amounts of light.

Step 2

Subject control groups to assigned temperatures for 7 days: 4°C daytime / 0°C nightime or 12°C daytime / 8°C.

Step 3

Immediately after each completed temperature treatment, collect 0.5g chlorenchyma tissue & place into centrifuge tube(s).

Step 4

Extract simple sugars from tissue samples using 80% aqueous ethanol, then centrifuge to separate tissue & extracted sugar solution.

Step 5

Decant solution into a glass dish & evaporate at room temperature.

Step 6

Dissolve contents of glass dish in 1mL distilled water.

Step 7

Measure specific gravity (relative density) with a refractometer.
