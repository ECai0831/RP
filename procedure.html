Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1

Plant 5 Opuntia humifusa pads; maintain at 27°C daytime / 22°C nightime with a 16-hour daytime & PAR of 65 μmol ⋅ m-2 ⋅ s-1.

Step 2

Subject control groups to assigned temperatures for 7 days: 4°C daytime / 0°C nightime or 12°C daytime / 8°C.

Step 3

Immediately after each completed temperature treatment, remove skin from cladode & excise about 0.5g chlorenchyma tissue from the cortex for collection.

Step 4

Place tissue into centrifuge tube(s).

Step 5

Add 4mL 80% aqueous ethanol, then heat in a water bath at 80°C for 10 minutes.

Step 6

Centrifuge at 2500 rpm for 5 minutes.

Step 7

Decant liquid components into a glass dish for evaporation.

Step 8

Add 2.5mL 80% aqueous ethanol to the centrifuge tube(s), then heat in a water bath at 80°C for 10 minutes.

Step 9

Centrifuge at 2500 rpm for 5 minutes.

Step 10

Decant liquid components into the aforementioned glass dish.

Step 11

Repeat steps 8-10.

Step 12

Dissolve contents in glass dish with 1 mL of distilled water.

Step 13

Measure specific gravity (relative density) with a refractometer calibrated such that dH20 at 20°C has a specific gravity of 1.000.
